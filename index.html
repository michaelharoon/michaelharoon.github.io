About Michael

I am from Southern California. I grew up in a Coptic community, and I spent much time with Cambodian and African American friends as my neighborhood was predominantly occupied by them. Because of my childhood, I have insight to some of the cultures of Cambodia and SoCal African American traditions. I grew up on the same block that Artesia Blvd runs through. Those who are into hip-hop (especially into Kendrick Lamar) should know the rich history of Artesia Blvd!
