About Michael

I am from Southern California. I grew up in a Coptic community, and I spent much time with Cambodian and African American friends as my neighborhood was predominantly occupied by them. Because of my childhood, I have insight to some of the cultures of Cambodia and SoCal African American traditions. I currently attend UC Berkeley, and I am studying computer science and math. I transferred from Long Beach City College, where I had transformative experiences. There, I was challenged to become mature as the population was filled with adults and was shockingly diverse. I adapted to become a model student in how I behaved. I demonstrated patience, tolerance, and an open mind, which opened many opportunities for me.
